Bond Therapy (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy describes the information we collect when you use our website and services, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Account details you provide (name, email, phone number, password).
  • Therapist application materials (bio, credentials, CV, photo, certificates).
  • Booking details (date, time, session type, payment reference).
  • Messages you send through our contact form.
  • Basic technical data (IP address, browser, pages visited) for security and analytics.

How we use information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process bookings and communicate with you about them.
  • To review and verify therapist applications.
  • To respond to messages and provide support.
  • To detect and prevent abuse of the service.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share booking details with the therapist you book with, and only the data needed to fulfil that booking. We may share information with service providers (hosting, email) under appropriate confidentiality terms, or where required by law.

Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. You may close your account at any time.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including password hashing, encrypted transport (HTTPS) in production, and access controls on administrative tools.

Contact

For privacy questions, please use the contact form on our main site.
