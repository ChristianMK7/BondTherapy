Guide

How to choose the best therapist

A good match matters. These points help people choose with more confidence and less pressure.

What to look for

Pick a specialist whose training matches your concern, such as anxiety, grief, relationships, trauma, or medication support.
Check language comfort. Feeling understood in Arabic, English, or French can make the first session easier.
Look at location and availability so the appointment fits your routine.
Choose online for convenience or on-site for face-to-face support.

Good first-session signs

You feel listened to without judgment.
The therapist explains the process clearly.
You leave with a sense of calm, structure, and next steps.
You feel safe enough to return for another session.

Small steps still move you forward.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers for the most common booking questions.

Can I book online or on-site?
Yes. Each booking includes a consultation type selector so the user can choose an online session or an on-site meeting before checkout.
How are specialists sorted?
The list is automatically sorted from highest rating to lowest, so the best-reviewed specialists appear at the top.
How does the checkout box work?
After selecting time, appointment type, and payment method, the checkout area shows the appointment summary and lets the user confirm the booking.
Is this for emergencies?
No. This website is only for scheduled appointments. For a crisis or immediate danger, emergency support should be used right away.
What payment methods are accepted?
Payments are processed online only via Wish or OMT. No cash payments are accepted at the time of booking.
Can I reschedule or cancel?
Yes. Contact the specialist directly or reach us through the Contact page. Rescheduling is subject to the specialist's availability.
Our story

Our mission

Asking for help is hard. Finding the right help shouldn't be. We connect people across Lebanon with verified, licensed mental health professionals — therapists, psychiatrists, psychologists — through a single, gentle directory.

Who we are

Bond Therapy is independently operated from Beirut, Lebanon. We are not a clinical practice — we facilitate discovery and booking between clients and independent licensed professionals.

How we verify therapists

Every therapist applies through a structured form, uploads their CV and credentials, and is reviewed by our team before being listed publicly. We check licensing details and only approve verified professionals.
